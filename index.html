DOMAINE SAINT AMANT

Domaine Saint Amant is a gorgeous winery in the Rhone Valley, just 40 km North of Avignon. Its incredible setting in the heart of the Dentelles de Montmirail accounts not only for the view and surroundings, but also for a unique terroir: steep slopes, facing South-East, with a soil of Triassic marl and limestone.

This means our wines are unique in our region: the altitude makes them refreshing and tasty, with a tendency towards fruity bouquets. The traditional methods of grape cultivation are the Wallut family's priority, along with respecting the terroir.
